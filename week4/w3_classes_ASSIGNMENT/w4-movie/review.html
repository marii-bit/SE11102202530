Review

Marisa Wishart

April 18, 2025

Spider-Man Is one of the most iconic superhero movies of the early 2000s. It perfectly blends action, heart, and humor, telling Peter Parker’s origin story with emotional weight and spectacular visuals. The film takes time to show Peter’s struggle with responsibility, love, and loss.

Tobey Maguire’s performance is heartfelt and sincere, and Willem Dafoe delivers a chilling portrayal of the Green Goblin. The web-swinging sequences are exciting even by today’s standards, and Danny Elfman’s score is unforgettable. Spider-Man remains a fan favorite to this day for good reason.

Trivia

  • Willem Dafoe performed many of his own stunts in the Green Goblin costume.
  • The "upside-down kiss" scene was nominated for Best Kiss at the MTV Movie Awards.
  • The film held the record for the biggest opening weekend at the time of release.
